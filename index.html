thoughts

A while ago I was introduced to Sam Harris’ podcast, which put me also on a path towards meditation. Meditation not being an easy practice to follow and to incorporate into one’s life, I looked for ways to ease the transition to mindfulness by reading books. Meditations by Marcus Aurelius was the first and most significant – I still enjoy browsing it every now and then.

The thoughts page on this website is meant as a place to share reflections from great thinkers, quotes, and other items of importance to me.
